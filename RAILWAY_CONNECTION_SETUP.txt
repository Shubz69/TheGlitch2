STEP 1: GET ACTUAL VALUES FROM RAILWAY
1. Go to Railway Dashboard → Your MySQL Service
2. Click "Connect" or "Variables" tab
3. Look for "Public Network" or "Connection" section
4. You should see the ACTUAL hostname (not ${{RAILWAY_PRIVATE_DOMAIN}})
